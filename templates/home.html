What is ToScan? (joey was here 2.0)

ToScan (short for Terms of Service Scanner) is a tool that is meant to help users know if companies respect their privacy.


Go to ToS Scanner

Our Mission

The reason we created ToScan is to better inform people of what they really get into when they check the I Agree box. In this modern world, corporate giants take advantage of us at every turn. Corporations purposely sandwich vital points of information, such as what they do with our personal data and who they share it with, in long, arbitrary corporate jargon that no one will survive through reading. We believe that the everyday person deserves to know what really happens when they click that I Agree box and surrender to a corporation's terms of service. With this in mind, we designed ToScan as an easy to use tool to let users now exactly where their precious data is going and exactly who they can trust with it.

How it Works

ToScan works by meticuously scanning through a webpage and looking for keywords and phrases that could indicate an unethical practice, after which it gives it a score based on of how many infractions it contains in each of our four major categories:

  • Tracking

  • 3rd Party Data Sharing

  • Silent changes to the Terms of Service

  • Violations of Personal and Legal Rights

Just another note: Our service requires no accounts, collects no data, uses no cookies, and doesn't remotely infringe on the privacy of its users in any way!
