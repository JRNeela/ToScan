What is ToScan?

ToScan (short for Terms of Service Scanner) is a tool that is meant to help users know if companies respect their privacy.


Go to ToS Scanner

Our Mission

The reason we created ToScan is to better inform people of what they really get into when they check the I Agree box. In this modern world, corporate giants take advantage of us: the everyday person. Corporations purposely sandwich vital points of information in long, arbitrary corporate jargon. We believe that the everyday person deserves to know what really happens when we agree to a corporation's terms of service.

How it Works

ToScan analyses a page and looks for phrases that could indicate an unethical practice, and gives it a score based off of how many infractions it contains. ToScan searches in four areas: Tracking, Data sharing, Silent changes to the Terms, and violations to your personal rights
